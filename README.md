## About

At 19, I'm wrapping up an accelerated 3-year BS in Computer Science at the University at Buffalo and heading into a CS PhD at **Michigan State University**, advised by **Dr. Pang-Ning Tan**. My research sits at the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and real-world deployment — from clinical mHealth apps to graph neural networks for materials science.

I've contributed to **2 co-authored publications**, presented to clinical groups of doctors and nurses, and won a competitive **$2,500 PEARL Research Award**. I don't just train models — I ship them into environments where they run on real patients and real data.
